The Problem

Campus Dining Is Broken

Students waste time, miss nutrition goals, and navigate outdated systems with no real-time data.

⏰

Time Wasted

Students waste time hunting for nutrition data across clunky platforms like Nutrislice. "Checking Nutrislice takes way too long."

📊

No Real-Time Data

No live dining hall crowding info — leads to guessing, frustration, and missed classes. "I would love to see tables before I walk the entire hall!"

🥗

Unhealthy by Default

42% eat out multiple times a week; only 10% hit recommended daily fruits & vegetables. "I knew what I shouldn't eat — I still did, it was easier to access."

Task: Navigate campus dining to select and log meals aligned with nutritional needs — without wasting time or missing class.

"A mobile platform that eliminates nutritional blind spots and waiting-time uncertainty in campus dining halls — letting university students eat healthy, on time, every day."

💬

AI Chatbot

Natural language queries — ask "What's a high-protein vegan option at Baker under 500 cal?" and get instant answers.

📷

AI Meal Scanner

Photograph your meal tray. AI identifies food items and logs full nutrition data with zero manual entry required.

🚦

Live Crowding Alerts

Color-coded Green/Yellow/Red hall occupancy status updated within ±2 minutes of actual sensor change, with 99% uptime.

⚙️

"Set & Forget" Profile

Dietary restrictions auto-loaded from student records. Zero manual entry required at first login.

📈

Habit Dashboard

Weekly nutritional goal progress, streak tracking, and personalized dining recommendations powered by AI.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Schedule-aware alerts: "You have 20 min before class — Baker has low wait time and GF options today."
