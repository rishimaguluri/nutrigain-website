Step-by-Step Guide

Using Each Feature

1
👤

Set Up Your Profile

On first login, Nutrigain auto-syncs your dietary restrictions from your university's student records. No manual entry required. You can also set weekly nutrition goals.

💡 Pro tip: Set your calorie and protein targets in Profile tab to unlock personalized recommendations.
2
🚦

Check Live Crowding

The Home tab shows all nearby dining halls with Green/Yellow/Red busyness indicators updated within ±2 minutes. Tap any hall to see estimated wait time and today's menu.

🟢 Not Busy (<30%) 🟡 Moderate (30–70%) 🔴 Very Busy (>70%)
3
🗺️

Find the Nearest Hall

The Map tab shows you the closest dining halls and on-campus restaurants. Filter by distance, busyness, or dietary compatibility to find the best option before class.

4
💬

Ask the AI Chatbot

Type natural language questions like "What's a high-protein vegan option at Baker tonight?" or "How many swipes do I have left?" and get instant answers.

Example queries:
"Is anything gluten-free at Morrill tonight?"
"What dining halls are open right now?"
"Show me meals under 600 cal with 30g protein"
5
📷

Scan Your Meal

In the Scanner tab, take a photo of your meal tray or upload from gallery. AI powered by OpenAI Vision identifies each food item and logs full nutrition data automatically.

  1. Tap Scanner tab
  2. Take photo or upload from gallery
  3. Review identified items
  4. Confirm to sync to Tracker
6
📈

Track Your Progress

The Tracker tab provides a weekly nutrition summary showing calories, macros, and progress toward your goals. Earn streak badges for consistent logging.
