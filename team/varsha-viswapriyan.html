About Varsha

Varsha Viswapriyan is a computer science and engineering student at The Ohio State University with deep experience in full-stack development, AI integration, and system architecture. As the Engineering & Prototype Lead for Nutrigain, Varsha designed the technical blueprint for the entire platform.

Varsha's contributions ensured that Nutrigain's ambitious feature set was technically feasible and grounded in real APIs and infrastructure. From selecting the right tech stack to designing the database schema, Varsha translated product requirements into concrete engineering plans.

Key Contributions to Nutrigain
Designed the complete system architecture: Streamlit → FastAPI → AI services → PostgreSQL/Redis/Azure
Selected and evaluated all API integrations: Nutrislice, USDA FoodData Central, OpenAI Vision, GPT-4o-mini, Leaflet Maps
Led Sprint 1 planning focused on AI meal scanner, chatbot, and dietary profile sync
Developed the detailed design documentation including the UX flow diagram and system process flow
Defined the unit testing strategy and requirement verification plan (pytest, integration tests, load tests)
