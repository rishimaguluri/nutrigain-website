About Varsha

Varsha Viswapriyan is a first-year undergraduate student at The Ohio State University in the Integrated Business and Engineering (IBE) Honors Program, pursuing a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering. She is highly motivated to explore the intersection of business and engineering and has already demonstrated that drive through competitive hackathons and leadership roles.

As the Engineering & Prototype Lead for Nutrigain, Varsha designed the technical blueprint for the platform — selecting the tech stack, architecting the system, and defining the testing strategy that grounds Nutrigain in real, buildable infrastructure.

Designed the complete system architecture: Streamlit → FastAPI → AI services → PostgreSQL/Redis/Azure
Selected and evaluated all API integrations: Nutrislice, USDA FoodData Central, OpenAI Vision, GPT-4o-mini, Leaflet Maps
Led Sprint 1 planning focused on AI meal scanner, chatbot, and dietary profile sync
Developed the detailed design documentation including the UX flow diagram and system process flow
