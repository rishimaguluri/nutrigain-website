About Rishi

Rishi Maguluri is a Stamps Eminence Scholar at The Ohio State University pursuing Finance through the IBE Honors Program, with minors in Cognitive Science and Computer Science. He is passionate about solving problems at the intersection of business, technology, and social impact — exploring opportunities in management consulting, strategy, and the startup space.

As the Business & Market Analysis Lead for Nutrigain, Rishi built the financial and strategic foundation that makes Nutrigain not just a great product — but a viable business. His market sizing work revealed a $16.3M TAM across U.S. universities, and his institutional licensing model provided the clearest path to revenue.

Key Contributions to Nutrigain
Developed complete TAM/SAM/SOM analysis: $16.3M / $4.43M / $1.5M per year for institutional licensing model
Designed the OSU University Contract revenue model with tiered institutional licensing ($75K–$300K/yr)
Led the per-student pricing analysis: $7.27 (small), $5.71 (medium), $6.02 (large) institution tiers
Identified the B2B institutional licensing model as the primary revenue path over direct student subscription
Outlined the long-term anonymized dining trend data secondary revenue stream (FERPA-compliant)
