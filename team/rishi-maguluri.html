About Rishi

Rishi Maguluri is a student at The Ohio State University with expertise in business strategy, market analysis, and institutional sales. As the Business & Market Analysis Lead for Nutrigain, Rishi built the financial and strategic foundation that makes Nutrigain not just a great product — but a viable business.

Rishi's work in market sizing revealed a $16.3M TAM across U.S. universities, and his institutional licensing strategy provided a clear, low-friction path to revenue — the university pays, students get it free — eliminating the biggest adoption risk in the consumer subscription model.

Key Contributions to Nutrigain
Developed complete TAM/SAM/SOM analysis: $16.3M / $4.43M / $1.5M per year for institutional licensing model
Designed the OSU University Contract revenue model with tiered institutional licensing ($75K–$300K/yr)
Led the per-student pricing analysis: $7.27 (small), $5.71 (medium), $6.02 (large) institution tiers
Identified the B2B institutional licensing model as the primary revenue path over direct student subscription
Outlined the long-term anonymized dining trend data secondary revenue stream (FERPA-compliant)
