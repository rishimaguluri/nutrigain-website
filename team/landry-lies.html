About Landry

Landry Lies is an Honors Computer Science Engineering student at The Ohio State University in the Integrated Business and Engineering (IBE) Program. As the Product Strategy & Design Lead on Nutrigain, Landry brought together business thinking and design sensibility to shape the product's direction.

Landry's work was instrumental in defining Nutrigain's value proposition and positioning in the campus dining market. With a deep curiosity about what motivates student behavior, Landry led the initial user research phases that uncovered the pain points that became the foundation of the product.

Key Contributions to Nutrigain
Led competitive analysis of MyFitnessPal, Nutrislice, CollegeMacros, and Cronometer — identifying the gap in the market
Developed the Business Model Canvas (Version 3) including key partners, revenue streams, and cost structure
Co-designed the user persona "MAX" based on synthesized interview data
Contributed to the concept screening matrix and final selection rationale
Crafted the value proposition that drives Nutrigain's positioning: "Campus Dining. Smarter."
